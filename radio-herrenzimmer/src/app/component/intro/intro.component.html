Radio Herrenzimmer is a collection of mixes I'm creating in my very own room in the basement. Styles vary from Technoto Deep House and Tech House, Minimal and Drum & Bass. Click to read more or simply start listening now. My current setup consists of a Macbook Pro 2012 and Traktor Scratch A6, two 1210 turntables, the Traktor Kontrol X1 controller, a Pioneer DJM-250-K mixer and my 20 year old stereo system. I also use two Pioneer XDJ-700 players.

Currently the mixes are hosted on Soundcloud, Mixcloud (with some delay), and hearthis.at (even more delay). Radio Herrenzimmer is also available as iTunes podcast. Simply search for "herrenzimmer" in the iTunes store, locate the "Podcasts" section and subscribe by clicking on the Radio Herrenzimmer image.
